[0:00 – 0:15] THE HOOK
"Every year, over 2 million students appear for JEE, NEET, and EAMCET.
Most of them study hard — but they keep repeating the same mistakes.
Why? Because no one tells them WHY they're getting things wrong.
That's the problem we're solving."

[0:15 – 0:40] THE SOLUTION
"Introducing NEXUS COACH — an AI-powered exam preparation platform
that doesn't just give you questions... it diagnoses you.

When you take a practice test on our platform, our system builds your
personal 'Error DNA™' — a fingerprint of exactly which topics you're
weak in, and why. Conceptual gap? Calculation error? Careless mistake?
We know the difference."

[0:40 – 1:05] THE FEATURES
"Based on your Error DNA, NEXUS COACH automatically:
  ✦ Generates a personalized 7-day AI revision plan using Gemini AI
  ✦ Recommends curated study videos and NCERT PDFs for YOUR weak topics
  ✦ Gives you a 24/7 AI Tutor that answers doubts in a Socratic style —
    never just giving you the answer, but making you think.

And every new test you take? Your plan adapts automatically."

[1:05 – 1:20] THE TECH / DIFFERENTIATION
"We built this with React, Node.js, and Google Gemini 2.5 Flash.
Unlike Byju's or PW which push the same content to everyone,
NEXUS COACH is the only platform where no two students
ever have the same study path."

[1:20 – 1:30] THE CLOSE
"Your mistakes are your roadmap.
NEXUS COACH turns them into your biggest advantage.
Thank you."
